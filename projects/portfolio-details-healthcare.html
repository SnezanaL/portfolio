Project details

As the developer for Healthcare Central Co.'s website, I assumed a central role in crafting an online presence that aligns with the company's innovative vision. Responsibilities included leveraging WordPress, HTML, and CSS to design and implement a user-friendly and visually compelling website. Ensuring seamless navigation and responsiveness, I aimed to effectively showcase the mobile health management system and companion app, providing a platform for individuals and healthcare enthusiasts to explore and engage with the company's offerings.
